2. Bhindi Fry (Crispy Okra Fry):

Ingredients:

250 grams lady's finger (okra), washed and dried
2 tablespoons oil
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1 teaspoon red chili powder
1 teaspoon coriander powder
Salt to taste
Chaat masala (optional, for garnish)
Instructions:

Trim the ends of the lady's finger and slice them vertically into thin strips.
Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
Add the sliced lady's finger to the pan. Sprinkle turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt.
Mix well and cook on medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the lady's finger is cooked and crispy, about 15-20 minutes.
Once cooked, sprinkle chaat masala (if using) for extra flavor.
Serve hot as a side dish with dal and rice or as a snack.
