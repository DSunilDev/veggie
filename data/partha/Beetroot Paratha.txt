Beetroot Paratha:

Ingredients:

 Beetroot, whole wheat flour, cumin seeds, coriander powder, salt, oil.


Instructions:

 Grate beetroot and mix it with whole wheat flour, cumin seeds, coriander powder, salt, 

and a little oil to form a dough. Make small balls of the dough and roll them out into parathas. Cook them on a hot griddle with a little oil until golden brown on both sides. Serve hot with yogurt or pickle.
