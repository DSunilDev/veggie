beetroot Halwa:

Ingredients: Beetroot, milk, sugar, ghee, cardamom powder, chopped nuts (like almonds, cashews, pistachios).


Instructions: 


 Grate beetroot and cook it with milk until soft. Add sugar and cook until it thickens. 


In another pan, heat ghee and fry the chopped nuts until golden.

 
Add this to the beetroot mixture along with cardamom powder. Cook until the mixture reaches a halwa consistency. Serve warm.
