Onion Tomato Chutney:

Ingredients:

 Onions, tomatoes, dried red chilies, garlic cloves, tamarind paste, oil, mustard seeds, curry leaves, salt, water.

Instructions:

Roughly chop onions, tomatoes, and garlic cloves.


Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Once they start spluttering, add dried red chilies and curry leaves.

Add chopped onions and garlic to the pan and saut√© until the onions turn translucent.

Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they are soft and mushy.

Allow the mixture to cool slightly, then transfer it to a blender. Add tamarind paste and salt. Blend until smooth, adding a little water if needed.

Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl and serve as a condiment with dosa, idli, or any Indian bread.
